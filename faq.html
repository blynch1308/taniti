Frequently Asked Questions

What currency is used on Taniti?

Taniti uses the U.S. dollar (USD) as its currency. Many businesses will also accept euros and yen. Several banks facilitate currency exchange, and major credit cards are accepted at many businesses.

What type of power outlets are used?

Power outlets are 120 volts, which is the same as in the United States.

What are the laws regarding alcohol?

Alcohol is not allowed to be served or sold between the hours of midnight and 9:00 a.m. The official drinking age is 18, but this is not always strictly enforced.

Is English widely spoken?

Many younger Tanitians, especially in tourist areas, speak fluent English. However, very little English is spoken in rural areas, particularly by older residents.

What are the medical facilities like?

There is one hospital and several clinics on the island. The hospital is well-equipped and has many multilingual employees to assist visitors.

Is Taniti a safe destination?

Violent crime is very rare. However, as tourism increases, there are more reports of pickpocketing and other petty crimes. Visitors should remain aware of their surroundings and secure their valuables.

What should I know about national holidays?

Taniti enjoys a large number of national holidays. On these days, many tourist attractions, restaurants, and shops will be closed. It is highly recommended to check the holiday schedule and plan accordingly.
