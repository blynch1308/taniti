Lodging & Dining

Find the perfect place to stay and eat. Taniti offers a wide variety of lodging and dining options to fit every budget and travel style.

Accommodations

  • Large Four-Star Resort

    Experience luxury at Taniti's one large, all-inclusive four-star resort.

  • Family-Owned Hotels

    Stay in small, locally-owned hotels for an authentic Tanitian experience.

  • Bed & Breakfasts

    Enjoy one of the island's growing number of charming B&Bs.

  • Inexpensive Hostel

    A friendly and inexpensive hostel is available for budget-conscious travelers.

Food & Dining

  • Restaurants

    Taniti has 10 restaurants: 5 serving local fish and rice, 3 serving American-style meals, and 2 serving Pan-Asian cuisine.

  • Grocery Stores

    Stock up on supplies at two supermarkets, two smaller grocery stores, or a 24-hour convenience store.

A Commitment to Quality

All restaurants and lodgings on Taniti are strictly regulated and regularly inspected by the Tanitian government, ensuring a safe and high-quality stay for all visitors.
