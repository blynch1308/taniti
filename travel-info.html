Getting to Taniti

  • By Air (Most Common)

    Almost all visitors arrive by air. Taniti's small airport can accommodate small jets and propeller planes and is currently being expanded to allow larger jets within a few years.

  • By Sea

    A small cruise ship docks in Yellow Leaf Bay for one night per week, offering an alternative way to arrive on the island.

Getting Around the Island

  • Public & Private Buses

    Public buses serve Taniti City from 5 a.m. to 11 p.m. daily. Private buses are available to serve all other areas of the island.

  • Taxis & Rental Cars

    Taxis are readily available in Taniti City. You can also rent cars from a local agency located conveniently near the airport.

  • Bike Rentals

    Bikes and helmets are available to rent from several vendors. Helmets are required by law for all riders.

  • On Foot

    Taniti City is fairly flat and very walkable. The popular Merriton Landing area is also compact and easy to explore on foot.
