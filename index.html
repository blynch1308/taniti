Entertainment

While Taniti is famous for its beaches, rainforest, and volcano, the island also features a wide variety of other entertainment options, many of which are located in the developing Merriton Landing area.

Sightseeing

Most tourists spend most of their time in Taniti City, which boasts native architecture and nearby white, sandy beaches that encircle Yellow Leaf Bay. Other popular activities include boat or bus tours of the island, hikes in the rainforest, or visits to Tanitis's active volcano.

Comfort & Cuisine

Rest easy in the perfect spot, from a 4-star resort to a budget-friendly hostel. Then, explore 10 unique restaurants serving local seafood, American, and more.
