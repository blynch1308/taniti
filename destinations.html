While many come to Taniti for the pristine beaches, lush rainforest, and majestic volcano, the island offers a vibrant and diverse array of activities for every type of traveler.

Many of these activities are located in Merriton Landing, a rapidly developing area on the north side of Yellow Leaf Bay.

Adventure & Tours

  • Rainforest Zip-Lining

    Soar through the lush jungle canopy for a thrilling bird's-eye view.

  • Snorkeling

    Explore vibrant coral reefs in crystal-clear waters.

  • Helicopter Rides

    Get an unforgettable aerial view of the volcano and coast.

Nightlife & Culture

  • Pubs & Microbrewery

    Enjoy local craft beer at the island's microbrewery.

  • Dance Club

    Dance the night away at Taniti's new and energetic club.

  • Museums & Galleries

    Discover Taniti's rich history or browse local art.

Casual Fun

  • Bowling & Arcade

    Perfect for a rainy day or a fun night out with the family.

  • Chartered Fishing

    Book a tour for a chance to catch big game fish.

  • Golf Course (Coming Soon)

    A new nine-hole course, expected to be operational next year.
